Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Pasta, Guanciale, Eggs, Pecorino

A rich, creamy Italian classic with crispy guanciale and a velvety egg sauce.

Full Recipe:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano
  • Black pepper

Cook pasta. Render guanciale. Mix eggs & cheese. Combine all quickly with pasta water for a creamy sauce.

Irresistible Chocolate Chip Cookies

Butter, Sugar, Flour, Chocolate Chips

Chewy, gooey, and packed with chocolatey goodness.

Full Recipe:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg, vanilla, flour, baking soda, salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Cream butter/sugars. Add egg/vanilla. Mix in dry ingredients, then chips. Bake until golden.

Aromatic Thai Green Curry

Coconut Milk, Chicken, Green Curry Paste

A fragrant and spicy curry with tender chicken and fresh vegetables.

Full Recipe:

  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 200g chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • Vegetables (bell peppers, bamboo shoots)
  • Fish sauce, sugar, basil leaves

Sauté paste, add chicken and cook. Stir in coconut milk and veggies. Simmer until cooked through. Season and add basil.
